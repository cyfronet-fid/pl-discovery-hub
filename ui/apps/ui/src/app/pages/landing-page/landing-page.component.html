Related Initiatives

eosc.gov.pl
EOSC.gov.pl is an initiative of the Polish EOSC partnership aimed at promoting and supporting the development of open science in Poland. It provides information on national activities related to open science and serves as a platform for exchanging experiences and best practices among Polish institutions involved in EOSC projects. The website is coordinated by the National Science Centre, which oversees the national implementation of the initiative.
Check
EOSC Beyond
EOSC Beyond is a project carried out within the framework of the European Open Science Cloud, designed to foster collaboration among researchers across Europe. Through it’s advanced infrastructure, scientists gain access to tools that facilitate and enhance research activities. The primary objective of EOSC Beyond is to introduce new features to the EOSC Core platform, enabling research service providers to access, integrate, and share research resources more efficiently.
Check
EOSC Association
EOSC Association brings together institutions and organisations committed to building an open scientific ecosystem in Europe. It works to improve access to research data, tools, and services while fostering collaboration between countries and research institutions. The Association also coordinates the development of common standards and infrastructure to ensure sustainable and open access to scientific resources for all users.

National Node
EOSC.PL

The Polish Open Science Platform is part of the European Open Science Cloud (EOSC) initiative, which aims to develop open and accessible research resources on a European scale. As one of the national nodes, Poland supports researchers by providing access to data, publications, and infrastructure that facilitates advanced research and international collaboration. National, regional, and thematic EOSC nodes, including the Polish Open Science Platform, serve as access points to the EOSC Federation, offering both proprietary and external services, such as data management and sharing.
